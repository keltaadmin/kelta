# KELTA Enterprise Platform
# Project Constitution

Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Effective Date: 2026-07-01

---

## Purpose

This constitution defines the engineering principles that govern the KELTA Enterprise Platform.

These principles apply to every module, feature, work package and future contributor.

---

# Principle 1
Architecture Before Implementation

Every significant feature shall be designed before it is implemented.

---

# Principle 2
Build Integrity

The repository shall remain in a buildable state.

Broken builds shall not be committed intentionally.

---

# Principle 3
Single Source of Truth

Business rules shall exist only once.

Duplicate implementations are prohibited.

---

# Principle 4
Configuration Over Hardcoding

Configurable behaviour shall always be preferred over hardcoded values.

---

# Principle 5
Documentation Evolves With Code

Every major architectural change shall be reflected in documentation.

---

# Principle 6
Enterprise Quality

Solutions shall prioritize maintainability, scalability and security.

---

# Principle 7
Traceability

Every major change shall be traceable through:

- Phase
- Work Package
- ADR
- Git Commit
- Release Notes

---

# Principle 8
Continuous Improvement

Engineering standards may evolve but shall remain backward compatible whenever practical.

---

This constitution is the highest engineering governance document of the KELTA Enterprise Platform.
